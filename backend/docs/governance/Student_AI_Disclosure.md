# Student AI Disclosure Notice

**For Students Using ZimPrep**

---

## How ZimPrep Uses Artificial Intelligence (AI)

ZimPrep uses **artificial intelligence (AI)** to help mark your practice exam answers and provide you with feedback. This notice explains how AI works in ZimPrep and what it means for you.

---

## What AI Does in ZimPrep

When you submit a practice exam answer, here's what happens:

### 1. **AI Reads Your Answer**
- The AI system reads your written or typed answer
- If you uploaded handwriting, AI converts it to text first

### 2. **AI Finds Official Marking Guides**
- The AI searches for **official ZIMSEC marking schemes** related to your question
- It only uses **verified examiner reports** and **model answers**

### 3. **AI Suggests a Mark**
- Based on the marking scheme, the AI suggests how many marks you should get
- The AI explains **why** it gave you that mark

### 4. **AI Gives You Feedback**
- The AI generates feedback similar to what a ZIMSEC examiner would write
- It tells you what you did well and what you can improve

---

## What AI Does NOT Do

It's important to understand what AI **cannot** do:

❌ **AI does not make final decisions** – All AI marks are checked by validation rules  
❌ **AI does not predict your real exam results** – Practice marks are not guarantees  
❌ **AI does not replace teachers** – You should still study with your teachers  
❌ **AI does not decide if you pass or fail** – Only real ZIMSEC exams do that  

---

## How We Make Sure AI Is Fair

### ✅ Every AI Mark Is Checked
- A special **validation system** checks every mark the AI gives
- If the AI makes a mistake, the mark is **automatically rejected**

### ✅ AI Uses Official Standards
- AI only follows **ZIMSEC marking schemes**
- AI cannot "make up" marks or rules

### ✅ Same Answer = Same Mark
- If you submit the same answer twice, you'll get the **same mark every time**
- AI does not randomly change marks

### ✅ No Bias Against Handwriting
- AI does not penalize you for messy handwriting (as long as it's readable)
- The marks are based **only on your content**, not how neat it looks

## Your Rights

As a ZimPrep student, you have the right to:

### 📌 Know When AI Is Used
- You will **always be told** when AI marks your work
- Practice results will clearly say "AI-assisted marking"

### 📌 Understand Your Marks
- You can see **why** the AI gave you a certain mark
- Feedback explains which parts of the marking scheme you met

### 📌 Appeal a Mark You Think Is Wrong
- If you believe the AI made a mistake, you can **request a review**
- A human will check the AI's decision (the AI will **not** mark it again)

### 📌 See Records of Your Work
- Your school can access records showing how your answer was marked
- This includes what marking scheme the AI used

---

## What Happens to Your Answers

### 🔒 Your Answers Are Private
- Your answers are **not shared** with other students
- Your answers are **not used to train** the AI (we don't teach the AI using your work)

### 🔒 Your Answers Are Stored Safely
- Your work is stored **securely** and encrypted
- Only you, your school (if applicable), and authorized ZimPrep staff can see your work

### 🔒 Your Answers Are Kept for Records
- Your practice exam records are kept for **educational purposes**
- You can ask to see or delete your data (see [Data Retention Policy](Data_Retention_Policy.md))

---

## How to Get the Best Results from AI Marking

### ✍️ Write Clearly
- If uploading handwriting, make sure it's **readable**
- The AI needs to understand your words to mark them

### 📝 Answer Fully
- Write complete answers based on what the question asks
- The AI checks if you've met **all parts** of the marking scheme

### 🎯 Use ZIMSEC Standards
- The AI marks based on **ZIMSEC rules**, so follow those standards
- Check past papers and marking schemes to see what examiners expect

---

## What If AI Makes a Mistake?

Sometimes AI can make errors. Here's what happens:

### 🛑 Automatic Checks Catch Most Mistakes
- If the AI's mark doesn't make sense, our system **automatically rejects** it
- You'll be notified if your answer needs manual review

### 🛑 You Can Report a Problem
- If you think the AI gave you the **wrong mark**, report it via the platform
- A human will review your concern

### 🛑 We Fix Problems Quickly
- If we find a pattern of AI mistakes, we **fix the system** and review affected students

## Important Reminders

### ⚠️ ZimPrep Is for Practice Only
- ZimPrep helps you **prepare** for ZIMSEC exams
- Your ZimPrep marks **do not count** toward your official results

### ⚠️ AI Helps, But You Must Study
- AI feedback is a **guide**, not a replacement for learning
- You still need to study, attend classes, and do your assignments

### ⚠️ Real Exams Are Marked by Humans
- Your actual ZIMSEC exams are marked by **human examiners**, not AI
- ZimPrep just helps you practice in a way that's similar to the real exam
